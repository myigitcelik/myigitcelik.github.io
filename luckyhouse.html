Lucky House

Lucky House is a US-Exclusive service providing app which includes Painting, Help Moving, Cleaning, Minor Home Repairs, TV Mounting and Furniture Assembly.

Sign Up / Login

The client specifically asked me to design this app in such a way that people who are not signed up to the app can still see what this app provides them. These screens are appearing while you are not logged in, the navbar elements are limited and placing in an order is restricted.

Main Page

After logged in, you see the extra features the app provides like chatting and settings. All six services are presented here in the homescreen. The search bar is placed because the client wanted to add more services in the next versions.

Address

I tried to make the adding and removing address process simple as possible. With these four simple steps you can add the address via Google Maps or manually, and just one press of a button you can delete an existing one.

Creating an Order

The client specifically asked me to make the order creation process simple as can be, so that the user does not lose interest and get lost in all different pages just to make an offer. Here are six different pages of order creation and they all can be done in just one page.

Orders

After creating your offer, you can preview your order and track it's status. After the order is assigned with a worker, you can use the chat feature to make contact with the worker(s). After the proccess is complete there is a rating modal to rate the job and give them a feedback.
