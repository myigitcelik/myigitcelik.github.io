Like Transfer

Like Transfer is a logistics app that connects customers with service suppliers.

On boarding

This is the onboarding screen of Like Transfer mobile application.

This section provides basic information about how this app operates.

Register / Login

This application has 9 different account types and therefore 9 different register methods.

Different register screens were specifically requested by the customer. The section also includes a verification screen and a information modal about location permissions.

Homescreen / Profile

In the homescreen user can access almost every function that this app provides.

By tapping the button on the top left corner where user's profile picture is located, user can access their profile screen. They can see their account informations and access various features offered by the application such as their wallet, settings etc.

By tapping the edit button that's located on the top right corner they can edit their informations.

Account Switch / Settings

If the user wants to access the settings or wants to change their account type, they open these screens.

As mentioned above, this app has 9 different account types. If the user wants to change the current account type, they can simply select the account type and tap "Approve".

In the settings screen, they can modify the permissions or notification preferences by their choice. They also change their WhatsApp, Telegram and Viber numbers and change the language of the app.

Account Wallet / Payment

To make the payments inside the app, the user must deposit money into their wallet first. The wallet contains 3 different currencies such as Turkis Lira, Dollar and Euro.

They can trace their transactions within the wallet section and by tapping the "Deposit Money" button, they can make the deposit by entering their card informations.

Map Screens

Like Transfer is an application that makes a lot of use of the map view.

A lot of the functions are being made on the map screens such as finding an advert or a service, searching for a roadside service nearby or to find a member workplace to purchase fuel or other products.
